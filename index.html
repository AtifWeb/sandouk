    About The
    Waypay Protocol

    Waypay provides a decentralized financial asset which rewards users with a sustainable fixed compound interest model through use of it’s unique SAP protocol.

    Waypay delivers the industry’s highest fixed APY, paid every 15 minutes, and a simple buy-hold-earn system that grows your $Waypay portfolio in your wallet at a lightning fast pace.

    The Waypay
    Token

    Waypay is the native token in which interest rebase rewards are paid. Every token holder receives 0.02355% interest every 15 minutes just for holding Waypay tokens in their wallet!

    Read More

    Auto-Staking
    & Compounding

    Crypto’s Highest Paying Auto-Staking and Auto-Compounding Protocol, with the highest fixed APY in the industry of 382,945%! Interest rewards are compounded every 15 minutes for every BSC wallet holding any $Waypay tokens.

    Read More

    Waypay Insurance
    Fund (SIF)

    The SIF serves as an insurance fund to achieve price stability and long-term sustainability of the Waypay Protocol by maintaining a consistent 0.02355% rebase rate paid to all $Waypay token holders.

    Read More

    Waypay Auto-Liquidity
    Engine (SALE)

    Liquidity allows for anybody to buy & sell their Waypay/BNB at anytime, however the less money/liquidity there is in the pool, the worse price you get. SALE adds more liquidity to that pool by itself.

    Read More

    The Waypay
    Treasury

    The Treasury supports the SIF in the event of an extreme price drop in the $Waypay token. The Treasury also funds investments, new Way pay projects and marketing for Waypay.

    Read More

    The Waypay
    Fire Pit

    2.5% of all $Waypay traded are burnt in the Fire Pit. The more that is traded, the more get put into the fire, causing the fire pit to grow, reducing the circulating supply and keeping the Waypay protocol stable.
