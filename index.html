    FAQ

    How do I get started?

    Check out our Pre-Sale info tab for more information. The token after presale will be available for purchase on our website and pancakeswap.

    What will I get with my tokens?

    $WPAY tokens will be your best investment yet. They will be automatically staked and compounded to your wallet at 1% interest a day with an APY of up to 5,000%.

    What is a reward yield?

    A reward yield is a percentage by which your staked $WPAY balance increases on the next rebase. You can find more info on our DAPP about the next reward yield for your $WPAY.

    How do you ensure that the price doesn't get manipulated at launch?

    There will be mechanisms hard-coded into our smart contract that will eliminate contract sniping bots and front-run snipers. In addition to this mechanism, there will also be a sales tax of 40%, which will decrease to the normal 15% on the 7th day and a max sell of up to 5% a day of your total tokens and to a maximum Value of $3000 (subject to terms).

    Who are the WayPay team?

    WayPay has a large team mainly built up of developers, project and marketing managers and crypto experts! We even got a crypto enthusiast on board to be the face of WayPay (Crypto OG) to help be the voice and liaison between WayPay and the community while we the founders concentrate on making this project the best there is on the market.

    How can you sustain the high APY?

    We have members of the team that monitor this, and we will have multiple income streams, not just the token sale to make sure the project sustains itself.
    we also have a set-up where we can inject additional liquidity when we need to which will maintain protocol stability and make sure the APY is upheld for the entire life of the project.
    On top of this, we have a model in place where after the first 12 months the interest rate reduces lower over time allowing for our protocol to be stable.

    What is a rebase?

    Rebasing is a mechanism by which your $WPAY balance increases automatically. When new $WPAY is minted by the protocol, it goes to the investors staking $WPAY.

    Is WayPay audited? KYC?

    Our team are doxed and the contract is just going through some changes but will have a full audit before launch.

    Why does WayPay look like other projects?

    Yes, you will see some similar designs options and features, and this is simply because before we built WayPay we wanted to take the best parts of the other protocols on the market and create the best one, Like a super version, so this is why we did not try and recreate the wheel and took what had been tried tested and proven and added it to our project.
